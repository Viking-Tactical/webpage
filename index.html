About Us

Tactical.....Vikings?

Viking Tactical - Founded on Comradery

Originally founded as a Call of Duty trick-shotting team in 2011, Viking Tactical (VTAC) as a clan was built on the core concept of comradery. Community and teamwork are at the center of VTAC. We value working together more than individual performance.

  • Lead by mature, experienced individuals.
  • Accepts and includes members from all around the world.
  • A decade of service.

We are a gaming clan that offers a sense of belonging and community no matter what kind of games you are interested in. We have speciality divisions where you can discuss specific games and genres of games with like-minded members. We do however, primarily focus on tactical military shooters and strategy games.

Features

What can VTAC offer you?

In-depth Ranking Structure

Whether you want to be a Private as a cog in the machine, or rank up to a Sergeant giving orders, we've got you covered.

Active Community

With an active Discord server, whatsapp groups, and more, you'll never feel alone!

Awards and Medals

Recognizing good service and outstanding comradery through awards and medals within the clan.

PC and Console players

As a multi-game, multi-platform clan, we welcome all gamers, no matter what hardware they play on!
