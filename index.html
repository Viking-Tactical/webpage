About Us

Tactical.....Vikings?

Viking Tactical - Founded on Comradery

Originally founded as a Call of Duty trick-shotting team in 2011, Viking Tactical (VTAC) as a clan was built on the core concept of comradery. Community and teamwork are at the center of VTAC. We value working together more than individual performance.

  • Lead by mature, experienced individuals.
  • Accepts and includes members from all around the world.
  • A decade of service.

We are a gaming clan that offers a sense of belonging and community no matter what kind of games you are interested in. We have speciality divisions where you can discuss specific games and genres of games with like-minded members. We do however, primarily focus on tactical military shooters and strategy games.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is there a minimum age to apply to VTAC?

    We have a strict policy requiring all members to be at least 16 years of age.

  • I don't have a microphone, can I still join?

    We prefer all members to have a working headset and microphone, though it is not explicitly required

  • I'm not from the United States, can I still be a member?

    Of course! We accept members from all around the globe! The only stipulation is that all members must be fluent or at least semi-fluent in English.

  • How do I join VTAC?

    You can apply via the link in the navbar of this webpage! Alternatively, you can click here to apply

  • Can I take a look at the clan before applying?

    We have a public discord that you may join before becoming an official member. You will be classified as a "Recruit" and will have limited permissions, but will be able to see what we're all about before you decide to apply for full-membership.

  • How does the ranking system work?

    After an accepted application, members are enlisted as a Private, but have lots of opportunities to rise through the ranks! Keep in mind however, that once you are promoted past the enlisted ranks and become part of Sub-Command, you will have several new responsibliities and work to do in order to help us keep the clan running smoothly!
